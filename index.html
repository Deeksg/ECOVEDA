Recycling Guide

Learn how to recycle and reduce waste effectively:

  • Paper: Recycle office paper, newspapers, and cardboard.
  • Plastics: Check for recycling codes 1-7 and follow guidelines.
  • Glass: Separate clear, brown, and green glass for recycling.
  • Metal: Recycle aluminum cans, steel, and copper items.

Waste Management Tips

Reduce waste and protect the environment by following these tips:

  • Composting: Turn organic waste into rich compost for gardening.
  • Reuse: Use reusable bags, bottles, and containers to minimize waste.
  • Donate: Give away old clothes, electronics, and furniture instead of discarding them.
  • Repair: Fix broken items rather than immediately replacing them.
